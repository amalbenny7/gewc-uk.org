Meet the visionary leaders guiding Greater Evangelism World Crusade UK in faith, service, and integrity.

Apostle Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere

Founder & Visionary Leader (1944–2014)

Apostle Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere (1944–2014) was a towering figure in the history of Pentecostal Christianity in Nigeria and beyond. A man of rare spiritual depth, fiery conviction, and remarkable humility, he devoted his life to preaching the uncompromised Word of God and establishing indigenous evangelism across Africa and other parts of the world. Through his ministry, Greater Evangelism World Crusade (GEWC), he ignited a movement that transformed countless lives and left an indelible mark on the body of Christ.

Born in 1944 in Nigeria, Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere experienced a divine encounter that set him on a lifelong mission to win souls for Christ. His early years were marked by a fervent passion for the Gospel and a deep sense of calling to reach the unreached. At a time when Christianity, especially Pentecostalism, faced opposition in certain regions of Nigeria, he responded to the call of God with courage and total surrender.

In 1969, Apostle Numbere founded Greater Evangelism World Crusade (GEWC), a ministry birthed out of prayer, sacrifice, and a burning passion for soul-winning. What began as a small outreach in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, grew into a global movement with branches across Nigeria, Africa, the United States, and the Far East. Under his leadership, GEWC became one of the foremost indigenous Pentecostal ministries in Nigeria, pioneering evangelism in areas that were largely unreached by the Gospel at the time. His missionary approach emphasized local leadership, cultural understanding, and the empowerment of indigenous believers to spread the Gospel within their communities.

Apostle Numbere was known for his fiery preaching style and unwavering commitment to truth. He preached the “pure Word of God,” emphasizing repentance, righteousness, holiness, and the power of the Holy Spirit. Beyond his pulpit ministry, Apostle Numbere was admired for his humility, simplicity, and integrity. He lived a life of self-discipline and total dependence on God, faithfully supported by his wife, Pastor Dr. Nonyem Numbere.

Even after his passing in 2014, Apostle Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere’s legacy continues through the Apostle Geoffrey D. Numbere Foundation and Bible College, which equip ministers and leaders to carry forward his vision of holiness and evangelism. GEWC remains a vibrant ministry, holding firm to the values and mission established by its founder — to take the Gospel to the ends of the earth.

Pastor Isaac Valentine Olori

International Director, GEWC

Pastor Isaac Valentine Olori serves as the International Director of Greater Evangelism World Crusade. He was commissioned on 14 December 2014, following the transition of Apostle Numbere, to continue the divine vision of evangelism and holiness.

A graduate of Agricultural Engineering, Pastor Olori is a gifted teacher of the Word with deep spiritual insight and a calm but strong leadership style. Having worked closely with the founder for years, he embodies the humility, discipline, and focus that GEWC represents.

Under his leadership, GEWC continues to grow globally, strengthening believers and expanding its reach through evangelism, discipleship, and missions. He remains steadfast in advancing the vision of “The Whole World for Christ,” guiding the ministry into a new era of relevance and revival.

Pastor Festus Igbagiri

Trustee, GEWC UK

Pastor Festus Igbagiri has been actively involved in ministry for nearly five decades, passionately teaching God’s Word and mentoring believers across generations. A retired Consultant Paediatrician, he combines professional excellence with spiritual maturity, ensuring accountability and unity in church governance.

His heart for service, humility, and compassion continues to inspire the GEWC UK family. He remains committed to fostering a church community where prayer, holiness, and integrity are at the core of every believer’s walk with God.

Pastor Kabia Blessing Gbirigbe

Trustee, GEWC UK

Appointed as Trustee in 2008 by Apostle Numbere, Pastor Blessing Gbirigbe has faithfully served with diligence, compassion, and spiritual wisdom. A retired Registered Nurse, she brings a nurturing spirit and a passion for mentoring young believers in faith and godliness.

Her ministry reflects care, empathy, and leadership rooted in integrity. Pastor Gbirigbe continues to contribute to GEWC UK’s mission by strengthening women’s ministries and ensuring that love and unity remain the foundation of every church endeavour.

Pastor Ilami Igbagiri

Trustee, GEWC UK

A dedicated Christian for over five decades, Pastor Ilami Igbagiri is an avid Bible scholar and a steadfast teacher of God’s Word. Her ministry reflects strength, humility, and a commitment to transparency and spiritual growth within the church community.

A retired Community Pharmacist, she serves as a mentor and encourager to believers, upholding the values of stewardship and faithfulness that define the mission of GEWC UK.
