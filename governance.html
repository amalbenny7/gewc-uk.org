Meet the visionary leaders guiding Greater Evangelism World Crusade UK in faith, service, and integrity..

Apostle Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere

Founder & Visionary Leader (1944–2014)

Apostle Geoffrey Dabibi Numbere was a towering figure in the history of Pentecostal Christianity in Nigeria and beyond. A man of rare spiritual depth, fiery conviction, and remarkable humility, he devoted his life to preaching the uncompromised Word of God and establishing indigenous evangelism across Africa and other parts of the world.

In 1969, he founded Greater Evangelism World Crusade (GEWC), a ministry birthed out of prayer, sacrifice, and passion for soul-winning. Under his leadership, GEWC became a global movement pioneering evangelism and empowering indigenous believers to spread the Gospel.

Known for his fiery preaching and humility, Apostle Numbere emphasized repentance, holiness, and the power of the Holy Spirit. His life remains a testimony of obedience to God’s call, his impact enduring through the ministries and lives he transformed.

Pastor Isaac Valentine Olori

International Director, GEWC

Pastor Isaac Valentine Olori serves as the International Director of Greater Evangelism World Crusade, commissioned on 14 December 2014, succeeding Apostle Numbere. A scholar of Agricultural Engineering, he has faithfully continued the vision of evangelism, discipleship, and holiness established by the founder.

His leadership is marked by deep humility, wisdom, and a passion for soul-winning. He continues to strengthen believers and expand the ministry’s reach globally, inspiring spiritual revival and dedication to Christ’s mission.

Pastor Festus Igbagiri

Trustee, GEWC UK

Pastor Festus Igbagiri has been actively involved in ministry for nearly five decades, passionately teaching God’s Word and mentoring young believers. A retired Consultant Paediatrician, he combines professional excellence with spiritual integrity, ensuring accountability and unity in church governance.

His vision is to uphold a church of prayer, integrity, and excellence, where every member feels valued and empowered to serve God and humanity faithfully.

Pastor Kabia Blessing Gbirigbe

Trustee, GEWC UK

Appointed as Trustee in 2008 by Apostle Numbere, Pastor Blessing Gbirigbe has served faithfully with integrity, compassion, and diligence. A retired Registered Nurse, she is passionate about teaching, welfare, and mentoring the younger generation to live Christ-like lives.

Her commitment to transparency and unity continues to strengthen GEWC UK, reflecting the love and hospitality of Christ in every aspect of service.

Pastor Ilami Igbagiri

Trustee, GEWC UK

A dedicated Christian for over five decades, Pastor Ilami Igbagiri is an avid Bible scholar and teacher of God’s Word. Her ministry focuses on spiritual support, financial accountability, and stewardship for the church’s mission.

A retired Community Pharmacist, she embodies wisdom, humility, and faithfulness, working tirelessly to uphold the vision and integrity of GEWC UK.
